Nissan GTR

The Nissan GT-R, is a sports car and grand tourer produced by Nissan, unveiled in 2007. It is the successor to the Nissan Skyline GT-R, a high-performance variant of the Nissan Skyline. Although this model was the sixth-generation to bear the GT-R name, it is no longer part of the Skyline line-up. The GT-R is built on the PM platform, which is derived from the FM platform used in the Skyline and the Nissan Z models.

Toyota Supra

The Japanese company’s sports car series has been around since way back in 1978. Enough time to garner a cult following and for everyone to select a favorite. Although it needs more time to prove its mettle, some even think the contemporary GR Supra has the makings of a living legend. Others, on the other hand, have had too much driving/tuning/racing exposure to Supra’s Mk4 iteration. Those are the lucky ones if our own two cents are allowed. The rest of the world must contend with simpler popular culture shenanigans, from the Fast and Furious blockbuster variety to the virtual racing game kind.
